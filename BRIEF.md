## About

Kannapolis Glass & Glazing has been a fixture in Charlotte and the surrounding area since 1982.
With more than 40 years of experience as a glass company, you can be assured of receiving the
best quality and service. They handle all types of residential glass installation and travel out
of the immediate area to serve commercial glass customers. They are a full-service glass services
provider specializing in custom glass window and door installations.

## Design Direction

- Clean, professional, trustworthy aesthetic appropriate for an established local trade business
- Prominently feature the 40+ years in business as a trust signal throughout
- Phone number should be visible and tappable at all times on mobile
- Do not invent services, claims, or content not listed above

---

## Page Sections (in order)

1. **Hero** — Strong headline, tagline, "Since 1982" trust signal, prominent phone CTA button
2. **Services** — Clean card or grid layout using the services list above
3. **About** — The about copy above, keep it close to the original wording
4. **Project Gallery** — Photo grid using images from the `/images` folder
5. **Testimonials** — All three testimonials above
6. **Contact** — Form with these fields only: Name, Phone, Email, Service Type (dropdown of services above), Message

## Technical Requirements

- Single `index.html` file with all CSS embedded in a `<style>` tag
- Vanilla JavaScript only, no frameworks, no external dependencies
- Mobile-first, fast loading
- Footer must include: address, hours, phone, email, Facebook link, copyright

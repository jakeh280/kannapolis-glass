About Us

A Local Glass Company You Can Trust

Kannapolis Glass & Glazing has been a fixture in Charlotte and the surrounding area since 1982. With more than 40 years of experience as a glass company, you can be assured of receiving the best quality and service.

They handle all types of residential glass installation and travel out of the immediate area to serve commercial glass customers. They are a full-service glass services provider specializing in custom glass window and door installations.
